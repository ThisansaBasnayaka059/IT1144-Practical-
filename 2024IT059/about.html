The Tech Exhibition Center at the University of Vavuniya was established in 2024 with the vision of becoming the premier technology showcase facility in the Northern Province. Our center brings together cutting-edge innovations from around the world, making them accessible to students, researchers, and the general public. We believe that experiencing technology firsthand is the best way to understand its impact on our lives and future. The exhibition center spans over 5,000 square meters and features dedicated zones for robotics, artificial intelligence, gaming technology, virtual reality, and emerging technologies. Each zone is designed to provide interactive experiences that engage visitors of all ages and technical backgrounds. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators and technologists while educating the community about technological advancements.

We work closely with leading technology companies, universities, and research institutions to ensure our exhibitions feature the latest and most relevant technological developments. Our expert team of curators and technical staff continuously update our displays to reflect current trends and breakthroughs in technology. Beyond exhibitions, we offer educational programs, workshops, and seminars designed to deepen understanding of various technologies. School groups receive special guided tours tailored to their curriculum, while professionals can attend specialized demonstrations relevant to their fields. The center also serves as a networking hub where technology enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, and investors can connect and collaborate. Our commitment extends to making technology education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or economic status, through subsidized entry fees and community outreach programs.
